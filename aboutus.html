ABOUT US

GreenLoop is an innovative computer science initiative developed by 2nd PU students to address the growing challenges of urban waste management through a synchronized digital ecosystem.
By integrating a mobile application with a dedicated website, the project enables families to log and track their daily waste disposal patterns in real-time. This data is directly shared with the BBMP (Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike), streamlining collection logistics and ensuring municipal accountability. To encourage sustainable habits, the system features a gamified "Green Points" incentive program; households earn points based on the volume and segregation of their waste. These points are then linked to a student's profile, contributing to a "Cleanliness Score" within their school records, effectively bridging the gap between civic responsibility and academic recognition.
